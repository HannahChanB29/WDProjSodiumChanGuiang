WHY WE MADE THIS

The mission of Survival Guide is to help guide students through their school life and journey with useful tips, tools, and encouragement.

The inspiration behind Survival Guide comes from the experiences and lives of students, including the creators themselves. As students, we understand the struggles of academics and personal growth, which is why this platform exists—not just as a tool, but as a companion.

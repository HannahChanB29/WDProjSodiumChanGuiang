**Description:**
*Survival Guide: Student Edition* aims to serve as a friendly online companion for navigating school life — from tackling deadlines and managing stress, to keeping motivation alive and finding balance between academics and fun.

Whether you’re cramming for exams, struggling to stay focused, or just trying to keep your balance, this website reminds you that you’re not alone in the journey. Here, survival doesn’t mean perfection — it means progress, perspective, and finding little victories in the middle of chaos.

***Website mission and inspiration behind “Survival Guide”***

* The **mission** of *Survival Guide* is to be able to guide students through their school life and journey with this website and what we have to offer. Here, students are provided with tips and tools that will serve as their aid in navigating their school life.
* The **inspiration** behind *Survival Guide* comes from the experiences and lives of students, like us, the creators. As students, we experience and live through struggles academically and personally. With this, we aim and hope to be able to help fellow students with their struggles and journey altogether not just as a tool but as a companion.

***JavaScript Integration:***
1. **Study Planner / Task Tracker:** JavaScript will be used to create a Study Planner. This feature allows users to type and add their daily study tasks, which then appears as a list on the page.

2. **Pomodoro Timer:**
JavaScript will be used to create a Pomodoro Timer. This feature encourages productivity by allowing users to focus for 25 minutes, followed by a short break. The timer counts down dynamically, with Start, Pause, and Reset controls handled by JavaScript.
